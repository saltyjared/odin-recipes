Lemon Pepper Salmon

Description

This protein packed meal is so easy and delicious, you'll be left ask ing for more. Goes perfectly with rice, pasta, or even by itself!

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon filet
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon lemon pepper seasoning

Steps

  1. Season salmon filet with olive oil, salt, and lemon pepper seasoning.
  2. Heat at medium-high heat in pan for 6 minutes each side. Alternatively, heat in air fryer at 375 degrees F for 6-7 minutes each side.
  3. Remove from heat and let salmon rest for 2 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice or pasta and enjoy!
