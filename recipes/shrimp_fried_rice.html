Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

Did a shrimp fry this rice (har har)? This dish is sure to use up any leftover ingredients laying around and turn them into a comfor ting and delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups leftover rice
  • 2 eggs (in this economy???)
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 pound cooked shrimp
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat wok or pan over medium-low heat with vegetable oil. Add rice, vegetables, and shrimp together in pan.
  2. Add eggs and heat until scrambled and broken up.
  3. Add sesame oil and soy sauce. Season additionally with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Enjoy!
