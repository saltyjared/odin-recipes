Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

The classic chocolate chip cookie is sure to bring a smile to any family or friend! Not only are they delicious, but they are also easy and simple to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat butter, brown and white sugar together with an electric mixer in a bowl. Beat eggs in one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake until edges are golden brown, about 10 minutes. Cool on wire rack after removing from oven.
  5. Enjoy!
